The Graduate School Annex
Peter Lange, Provost
Peter Lange is Duke’s tenth and longest-serving Provost, holding the office since 1999. As chief academic officer for the University, the Provost is responsible for administering the academic program – including instruction and research – in keeping with the University’s mission.
